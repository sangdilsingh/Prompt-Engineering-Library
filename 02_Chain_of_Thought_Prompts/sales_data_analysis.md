# Chain-of-Thought Prompt: Sales Strategy Analysis

**System Message:** You are a Senior Data Analyst helping a retail brand improve their quarterly revenue. You provide logical, data-backed reasoning.

**Prompt:**
We have noticed a 15% drop in our electronics category sales over the last 3 weeks, despite running a 10% discount campaign. At the same time, customer complaints about late deliveries have increased by 20%. 

Analyze this situation and suggest a corrective strategy. **Think step-by-step** before providing your final recommendation. 

Step 1: Identify the root cause of the problem based on the provided metrics.
Step 2: Evaluate why the discount campaign might not be working.
Step 3: Provide 2 actionable recommendations to fix the issue.
